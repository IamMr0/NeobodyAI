# Project Brief: IRON AI – The Kinetic Gym Assistant

## 1. Vision & Core Value Proposition
IRON AI is a high-performance fitness ecosystem that bridges the gap between raw biomechanical data and actionable training intelligence. It leverages AI to provide real-time body analysis, hyper-personalized nutrition, and dynamic workout engineering, all wrapped in a bold, neubrutalist interface that reflects the raw energy of high-intensity training.

---

## 2. Target Audience
- **The Performance Architect:** Data-driven athletes who want to optimize every set and rep.
- **The Home Engineer:** Users training with limited equipment who need AI to maximize their available tools.
- **The Recovery-First Trainee:** Individuals focused on metabolic health and balanced, AI-optimized nutrition.

---

## 3. Core Features & User Stories

### A. AI Body Analysis (Biomechanical Command)
- **Feature:** 3D skeletal and muscular visualization with real-time biomechanical data.
- **User Story:** "As an athlete, I want to see exactly which muscles are peaking or fatigued so I can adjust my training volume safely."
- **Key Metrics:** Weight tracking, Body Fat %, Muscle Mass (kg), and Metabolic Insights.

### B. The Vault (Smart Exercise Library)
- **Feature:** A library of 500+ movements filterable by equipment (Dumbbells, Bodyweight, Barbell, etc.).
- **User Story:** "As a trainee, I want to find exercises that match my current setup while receiving AI recommendations for peak performance."

### C. Custom Workout Builder (Routine Engineering)
- **Feature:** Drag-and-drop workout construction with AI-driven optimization insights.
- **User Story:** "As a user with only resistance bands and a bench, I want the AI to engineer a routine that ensures I'm not neglecting my posterior chain."

### D. Fuel Up (AI Nutrition & Diet)
- **Feature:** Real-time macro tracking linked to workout intensity and glycogen levels.
- **User Story:** "As a recovering athlete, I want to know exactly what to eat post-workout based on my session's actual burn."

### E. AI Chatbot Assistant
- **Feature:** Conversational interface for troubleshooting plateaus and receiving form cues.
- **User Story:** "As a lifter hitting a plateau, I want to ask the AI for a 5x5 cluster progression to break through my current limit."

---

## 4. Design Direction: Kinetic Logic
The project uses a **Neubrutalist** aesthetic characterized by:
- **Color Palette:** High-contrast vibrance using Neon Yellow (#f4ff00), Electric Green, and Cobalt Blue against a stark #f9f9f9 surface.
- **Typography:** Space Grotesk (all-caps for headlines) to convey a technical, engineering-forward feel.
- **Visual Language:** 4px black borders ("thick"), flat shadows (4px offset), and un-rounded corners to emphasize stability and impact.

---

## 5. Technical Requirements
- **Device Support:** Mobile-first responsive web design with a dedicated "Command Center" desktop experience.
- **Data Status:** Encrypted, real-time sync between wearable sensors and the UI.
- **AI Integration:** LLM-driven chat and computer vision for 3D body mapping.
